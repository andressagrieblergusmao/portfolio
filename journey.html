FOREST WATER UP/ SOILING PROJECTS

  • Actively collaborated in the FORESTWATERUP/SOILING project, supporting the design and implementation of the Ecology Day experiment on Mediterranean tree species.
  • Participated in seed collection, preparation of nursery trays, and setup of the experimental design for four Mediterranean species (holm oak, cork oak, strawberry tree, and juniper).
  • Monitored seedlings weekly in the nursery, recording plant height and leaf number for all treatments and trays, covering several hundred individuals per species.
  • Evaluated different germination treatments for juniper seeds to assess their effects on emergence and early growth.
  • Performed destructive biomass assessments, separating and quantifying primary and secondary roots with ImageJ, and measuring leaf area to characterize above- and below-ground growth.
  • Assisted in sample processing (leaf maceration and preparation) for subsequent laboratory analyses of plant material.
  • Participated in transplanting the nursery-grown seedlings to field sites, contributing to the establishment of Mediterranean tree plots as a final stage of the Ecology Day initiative.
