Who is Andressa?

Andressa Griebler Gusmão is an Environmental Engineer with strong experience in data analytics, scientific research, and geospatial studies. Throughout her academic journey, she has worked with large datasets, developed analytical workflows, and applied tools such as SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and QGIS.

Her background in environmental systems allows her to merge technical, analytical, and scientific reasoning to extract actionable insights from complex data. She has published academic work, conducted experimental research, and collaborated on multidisciplinary projects focused on environmental monitoring and sustainable development.

Beyond her technical work, Andressa is passionate about translating complex problems into clear, visually compelling analyses. Her approach combines structured methodology, curiosity-driven exploration, and a strong foundation in statistical and geospatial analysis.
